What is Bevvi?

Bevvi is a pick-up only service for discounted alcohol. We have partnered with your favorite local liquor stores to provide exclusive members-only pricing, seasonal combos and the best offers on alcohol beverages to keep you stocked.

How does Bevvi work?

Bevvi sends you offers from your neighborhood stores through our app (available on iOS and Android) as well as email. The stores determine the length of the offers and the quantity of items available. Each offer lasts until the expiry date or until supplies run out. You can pre-order, pre-pay, and schedule the pickup date and time. You have a week from the date of purchase to pick up the offer. The order will be pre-packaged and waiting for you. Once you are at the store, you show your confirmation and the products are yours. Quick and easy!

Does Bevvi do deliveries?

Bevvi is a pick-up only service for discounted alcohol. We have partnered with your favorite local liquor stores to provide exclusive members-only pricing, seasonal combos and the best offers on alcohol beverages to keep you stocked.

How do I enter an order?

You can select your order with our user-friendly app and website. You can search your favorites, choose the quantity, schedule your pickup and prepay for the order in a few simple steps.

How do I know about the offers?

You will receive notifications from your neighborhood stores. Make sure push notifications are enabled on your app to receive the latest deals. You will also receive emails when offers become available.

How do I pay?

Pay using your credit card. Your card information will be safely and conveniently stored in your profile for quick, convenient checkout. When you arrive at your liquor store, your order is paid for and ready to go!

Can I pay by cash?

We do not accept cash as a method of payment. Payment is by credit card only because we pre-process the payment before you pickup the orders.

What happens if I don’t pickup the order when I schedule it?

It’s at the discretion of the store to keep your order available if you do not pick up at the time it was scheduled for. Neither Bevvi nor the store will be responsible for the loss of the goods ordered if not picked up at the scheduled date and time.

Can I order my favorite items even if they are not part of the special offers?

If the store offers their entire inventory, then you will be able to buy items that are not part of the special offer.

How does age verification work?

Age verification is done by the store at the point of pickup. If you cannot produce a legal ID proving you are at least 21 years old during the time of pickup, the store will not release your order and there is no refund available.

Whats the minimum age for people to use the app or website?

The minimum age to use Bevvi website and app is 21 years.

Where is Bevvi offered?

Bevvi is currently offered in New York City, New Jersey and Connecticut. We will soon be adding other states.

Can I cancel my account?

Do I get points for each order?

You get 100 points per dollar you spend.

Can the points be redeemed?

Yes! For every 10,000 points, you will be credited 10$ toward a future purchase.

What do I get for inviting my friends?
